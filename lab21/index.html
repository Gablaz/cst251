Lab 21: 2nd Draft Team Project Sketches

Feedback

I enjoyed presenting my quick sketches to my teammates, and I received some great feedback. The process really helped me think of some new ideas and refine my project sketches. My teammates seemed most excited about my “Art Gallery and Museum” themed idea. They liked the idea of frames surround our various images as well as the idea of displaying our contents in a gallery like format. They felt it could be a really creative and fun design for the website. My teammates were however less than impressed with my ideas for homepage layouts. We decided that my designs felt too cluttered and compressed, and they encouraged me to create more traditional and spread out homepages. My team also encouraged me to simplify some of my other designs and develop ways to make the site more user friendly with easier access to pages or different elements of the site.

Challenge

The purpose of this lab was create a second draft of sketches for our final project after receiving feedback on our previous sketches from our group members.

Problems

I had some trouble with getting started. It was a bit difficult for me to re-imagine and alter some of my designs to fit my team’s feedback and comments. Eventually, I was able to sketch the desired designs while keeping some of my original ideas and elements.

Results

I worked off my team’s feedback and further developed three of my best ideas from the previous lab. I enjoyed the process and feel that I was able to explore different ideas and possible layouts for my team’s final project.
