Lab 7: Intro to CSS

Challenge

The purpose of this lab was to create our Lab 7 Index and create a style sheet to style it with.

Problems

We had to experiment to figure out what looked good aesthetically to us. We had trouble figuring out how to style our classes and IDs but referred to W3 Schools for help. We also used the inspect feature to test different elements and refine our page layout.

Results

In the end we each created a stylized webpage complete with images, colored backgrounds, margins, and borders.
