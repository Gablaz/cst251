Lab 19: CSS Frameworks

Challenge

The purpose of this lab was to experiment with Bootstrap.

Problems

We had to experiment with Bootstrap in order to understand how it worked and functioned. It took some time to get used to the new styling method, and we referred back to the lecture and W3schools for assistance.

Results

We successfully styled the layout and certain appearances, such as the navbar, of our website with Bootstrap.
